Products

Every product we create adheres to the highest international standards

Production

At Baltic Industrial Composites, we specialize in crafting high-quality fiberglass structures for the chemical, petroleum, and energy industries. From tanks to gas purification equipment, gas ducts, and chemical process equipment, our products are designed to meet the highest international standards.
Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our adherence to standards such as AD-Merkblatt (Germany), EN13121 (Europe), ASME RTP 2000-1 (USA), and PLN-83 (Sweden). With cutting-edge technology and a skilled team, we ensure that every product we create not only meets but exceeds industry benchmarks.
Choose us for reliable and innovative solutions that are in line with international standards. Your satisfaction is our commitment, and we deliver quality that stands the test of time.
